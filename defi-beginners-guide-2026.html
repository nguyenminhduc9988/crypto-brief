What is DeFi and Why It Matters

DeFi (Decentralized Finance) is a $50B+ ecosystem of financial applications built on blockchain networks, primarily Ethereum and Layer 2s. It replaces traditional financial intermediaries—banks, brokers, exchanges—with open-source smart contracts. Anyone with an internet connection can lend, borrow, earn interest, trade derivatives, and buy insurance without permission, paperwork, or minimum requirements.

The DeFi Tech Stack

How to Get Started with DeFi

Getting into DeFi follows a clear path. Set up a non-custodial wallet (MetaMask for Ethereum, Phantom for Solana), fund it with ETH or SOL, then connect to a DeFi platform. Always verify contract addresses—rug pulls and scams are common—and never invest more than you can afford to lose.

1
Install a self-custody wallet (MetaMask / Phantom)
2
Buy ETH or SOL & transfer to your wallet
3
Connect to Aave, Uniswap or Lido
4
Start small, verify every contract address

Top DeFi Use Cases

DeFi spans lending, trading, yield generation and more. The biggest platforms let you put idle assets to work or trade without a centralized middleman.

🏦
Lending
Aave & Compound: earn 3–8% APY or borrow against collateral, no credit checks.
🔁
DEX Trading
Uniswap, Curve & Raydium swap tokens peer-to-peer, no centralized exchange.
🌾
Yield Farming
Supply liquidity to pools for trading fees + token rewards (5–50% APY).
⚡
Staking
Stake ETH to secure the network and earn 3–5% APR.
💵
Stablecoin Yield
Lend USDC/DAI for low-risk 4–8% APY.
🛡️
Insurance
Protocols like Nexus Mutual cover smart-contract hacks.

Risks and How to Manage Them

DeFi is powerful but unforgiving. Smart-contract bugs, impermanent loss, liquidations and regulation all pose real threats. Manage them by diversifying across audited protocols and keeping the bulk of capital in blue-chip DeFi.

❌ Reckless DeFi
  • •Putting everything in one unaudited farm
  • •Chasing 200% APY memes
  • •No wallet backups
  • •Borrowing near max LTV
  • •Ignoring contract audits
✅ Smart DeFi
  • •Diversify across Aave, Lido, MakerDAO
  • •Stick to audited, blue-chip protocols
  • •Keep 80% in established DeFi
  • •Track positions weekly
  • •Test with small amounts first
